Life is a sum of some memories and events. It is like an energy source. We can feel it, we are

 living through it, but we can’t objectify it. We think we are controlling it, while we are like sands

 in a desert in front of it. Scientifically, the definition of life is operational. They allow us to

 separate living things from non- living things. But they don’t actually pin down what life is.

 Life is cruel and humble at the same time. Life is bound to no one. We can’t control it. The best

 we can do is change the perspective of seeing it. Not all the events of life are satisfactory.

 Failure is one of the biggest parts of our life. It is upon us to choose the events we will act on.

 From the day of our birth, we start to chase it. We have no clue how far we will go. But we all

 take part in this non predictive run. We all see dreams, imagine ourselves as the king of our own

 kingdom. But there is a lot of difference between dreaming for success and capitalizing on them

in reality. Not everybody has the caliber to do that. Those who can find the meaning of it and

cope with it are the ones who write the success stories.

Life is a riddle. There is no shortcut to solve this. No one can copy others' paths to gain

 prosperity. Everyone gets different surroundings, environment, people and other living stuff.

 Everyone needs to solve this puzzle of life in their own way. The best one can do is to take

 motivation from their motive figure and use that in their own way. Self-motivation, hard work

 and focus are the three major things on the path of life. It is simple when one gives his best,

 irrespective of his surroundings. One word of life that demands is to focus on yourself. Another

 such element is hope. Hope is what gives us a way to survive. Survival and hope are important

 aspects for anyone and everyone as far as living is considered.
